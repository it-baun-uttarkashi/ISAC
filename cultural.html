Who We Are

About the Society

While science takes care of our materialistic needs, it is culture that makes us civilized.

Our Vision

While science and technology take care of our materialistic needs, it is the culture of the times which makes us civilized. We don't just learn about culture — we live it through our art, music, and theatre. We create it through our shared experiences.

Our Mission

Each student picks up at least one cultural activity, enriching their personality and contributing to the vibrant campus life.

Our Roles

  • Plan, schedule & organize intra-institute level events.
  • Select and prepare teams for external organization events.
  • Organize capsule workshops for students who want to learn a particular cultural activity.

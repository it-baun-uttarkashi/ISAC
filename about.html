About ISAC

The Institute Student Activity Council (ISAC) at Government Institute of Technology Baun is the apex body responsible for organizing and promoting all extracurricular activities on campus. We bridge the gap between academics and holistic development by providing students with opportunities to explore their talents, develop leadership skills, and build a vibrant campus community.

ISAC coordinates various clubs, organizes cultural and technical festivals, sports events, and community outreach programs. Our aim is to create a balanced environment where students excel not only academically but also in co-curricular and extracurricular domains.

Through ISAC, students learn teamwork, event management, communication skills, and leadership — essential qualities for professional success and personal growth.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at ISAC

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in all activities — from technical fests to sports championships — pushing students to give their best.

Collaboration

Every great event, workshop, or initiative is a team effort. We believe in the power of collective energy and mutual support.

Innovation

ISAC fosters creative thinking — through hackathons, idea challenges, and platforms where original thought is celebrated and rewarded.

Inclusivity

Every student deserves a platform. We strive to create an environment where every voice is heard and every talent is given a stage.
